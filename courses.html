Frequently Asked Questions

What is the CCIE Security Certification?

+
The CCIE Security Program covers core technology areas and is designed to validate end-to-end lifecycle capabilities in complex security technologies and solutions, from planning and design to operation and optimization. Aspects of automation and programmability are an integral part of the CCIE security concept.

What is the duration of the CCIE course?

+
You can select weekdays or weekend batches as per your convenience. You can opt for an onsite or online course. Regular weekday (Mon – Fri) classroom training takes 8 weeks or 120 hours while Weekend (Sat-Sun) classroom training takes 2 months or 120 hours. Please get in touch with us for the schedule, fees and other details.

Do you have weekend training for professionals?

+
Yes. For more information, please contact our team.

What are the recommended study materials for CCIE Security training?

+
The typical study material for CCIE security training is CCIE press books, latest workbooks prepared & revised regularly by experts.

How to prepare for the CCIE Security exam?

+
Preparation involves mastering both theory and practical labs. Study Cisco’s official guides, practice configurations and troubleshooting in labs, and take practice exams to assess your readiness and focus on areas needing improvement.

What job roles are available after CCIE Security certification?

+
Here are some job roles offered to CCIE Security Experts:
  • Network Engineer
  • Senior Network Engineer
  • Network Administrator
  • Network Security Engineer
  • Network Security Administrator
  • Network Security Specialist

What is the cost of CCIE Security certification?

+
The cost varies depending on the region and training provider. Contact our team for up-to-date pricing details.

Can I take the CCIE Security exam online?

+
The written exam can be taken at a certified Pearson VUE testing center, but the lab exam must be taken in person at a Cisco-certified lab location.

How long is the CCIE Security certification valid?

+
The CCIE Security certification is valid for three years, after which recertification is required to maintain active status.
