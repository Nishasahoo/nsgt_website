Live Sessions Services & Learning – FAQs

How are live sessions carried out? →

Live classes are carried out over Zoom Meetings. The Zoom ID is shared once the learner enrolls in the course and is added to the learner portal.

Live Classes & Missed Sessions? →

You don't need to worry if you miss a session. The recorded videos will be uploaded to the portal within 24 hours of the live session and will be available to learners.

Where can I enroll for this course? →

You may register yourself on nitizsharma.com

Why No Classroom Training? →
